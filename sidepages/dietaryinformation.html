Protein is king

To grow in size, you must be on a caloric surplus. This means you must eat more than you burn. So, if your goal is to build muscle, you must consume carbohydrates, protein, fats and fibers in a balanced diet.

Protein is the most important macronutrient for muscle growth. It is the building block of muscle tissue. You can find protein in foods like chicken, beef, fish, eggs, dairy products, legumes, nuts and seeds.

Carbohydrates are the body's main source of energy. You can find carbohydrates in foods like rice, pasta, bread, potatoes, fruits and vegetables.

Fats are essential for hormone production and overall health. You can find fats in foods like avocados, nuts, seeds, olive oil, coconut oil and fatty fish.

Fibers are important for digestion and overall health. You can find fibers in foods like fruits, vegetables, whole grains, nuts and seeds.
